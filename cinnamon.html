Cinnamon Balls

Cinnamon balls are delicious , healthy snacks with digestive properties. Kids love them and your stomach will too!

A further list of ingredients and Debbie's other recipes can be found at her site.

  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • vanilla
  • Dough Mixture
    • flour
    • baking soda
    • salt
    • cinnamon powder
    • water

Steps to make Cinnamon Balls!

  1. Mix dough, knead 5 times and cover to rest
  2. roll dough flat and layer with butter and cinnamon
  3. Broll dough into cylinder, chop slices and roll into balls
  4. Bake at 325 for 15 minutes
